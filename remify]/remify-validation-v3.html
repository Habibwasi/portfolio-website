🎯 3-Minute Script🎯 ৩ মিনিটের স্ক্রিপ্ট
  1. Context: "I'm mapping pains NRBs face managing money, family, and home from abroad."
  2. Money: "When you send money home, what goes wrong? How do you know it was used right?"
  3. Family: "How do you check parents' wellbeing or missed bills from abroad?"
  4. Home fixes: "If a repair is needed in Sylhet/Dhaka, how do you arrange it?"
  5. Stress: "What's the most stressful part?"
  6. Hypothetical: "If one service handled money categories + family check-ins + verified workers, how useful?"
✅ Interview Hygiene✅ ইন্টারভিউ নীতি
  • No app name or pitch. Stay in problem space.
  • Let them talk 80%. Capture verbatim quotes.
  • Tag segment: diaspora abroad, family in BD, or worker.
  • Ask for permission to re-contact for pilot.
